Overview

Why Naver Blog & Instagram?

Naver Blog

Naver is South Korea's dominant search engine with over 70% market share. Korean travelers routinely search Naver Blog for activity reviews, travel tips, and destination guides before making booking decisions. A well-structured Naver Blog post can drive consistent organic traffic for months.

Instagram

Instagram is widely used by Korean travelers for visual inspiration. Korean users actively search hashtags related to travel destinations and activities. A consistent Instagram presence builds brand trust and supports the Naver Blog content strategy.
